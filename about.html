Our History

Serving our community For years

Grace Community Church began in 1985 when a small group of 25 families gathered in a living room with a vision to create a church that would welcome all people and help them grow in their relationship with God.

From those humble beginnings, we've grown into a thriving congregation of over 1, 000 members, but our mission remains the same: to create a community where everyone can experience God's love and grace.

Throughout our history, we've remained committed to Biblical teaching, authentic worship, and compassionate service to our community and world. We're grateful for God's faithfulness and excited about what He has in store for our future.

1985

The Beginning

Grace Community Church is founded by Pastor John Reynolds and 25 families meeting in homes.

1990

First Building

The church purchases its first building, a small chapel on Main Street.

2000

Growth and Expansion

The congregation grows to 500 members, and we break ground on our current campus.

2010

Community Outreach

Launch of our food pantry, counseling center, and global missions program.

Today

Continuing the Mission

Over 1, 000 members, multiple services, and a thriving online ministry reaching people around the world.

What We Believe

Our foundational beliefs based on Scripture

At Grace Community Church, our beliefs are rooted in the historic Christian faith as revealed in the Bible. We affirm the essential doctrines that have united Christians throughout the centuries.

The Bible

We believe the Bible is God's Word, divinely inspired, without error, and our ultimate authority for faith and life.

God

We believe in one God who exists eternally in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus is fully God and fully man, born of a virgin, lived a sinless life, died for our sins, rose from the dead, and will return again.

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone, and not by human works or merit.

The Holy Spirit

We believe the Holy Spirit dwells in believers, empowering them for godly living and service.

The Church

We believe the church is the body of Christ, called to worship God, make disciples, and serve the world.

Join Our Church Family

How to become a part of Grace Community

We believe that being part of a church family is essential for spiritual growth. At Grace Community, we want to make it easy for you to get connected and find your place.

Here are some simple steps to help you become part of our church family:

1

Attend a Sunday Service

Experience our worship and teaching firsthand. We have services at 9:00 AM and 11:00 AM every Sunday.

2

Attend a Welcome Lunch

Join us on the first Sunday of each month after the 11:00 AM service to meet our staff and learn more about our church.

3

Join a Small Group

Connect with others in a smaller setting where you can build relationships and grow spiritually.

4

Attend Membership Class

Learn about our beliefs, values, and vision, and discover how you can be a part of our mission.
