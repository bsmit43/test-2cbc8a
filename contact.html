Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What time are your Sunday services?

▼

We have Sunday morning services at 9:00 AM and 11:00 AM, with an evening service at 6:00 PM. Our morning services feature a blend of traditional and contemporary worship, while the evening service has a more casual, contemporary feel.

Is there childcare available during services?

▼

Yes, we provide childcare for infants and toddlers (0-2 years) during all of our services. We also have age-appropriate children's programs for preschool (3-5 years) and elementary (K-5th grade) during our Sunday morning services.

How do I become a member of the church?

▼

We offer a membership class called "Discover Grace" on the first Sunday of each month after the 11:00 AM service. This class covers our church's history, beliefs, mission, and the benefits and responsibilities of membership. After completing the class, you can schedule a meeting with one of our pastors to discuss membership further.

How can I get involved in a ministry?

▼

We have many opportunities to serve in various ministries! You can fill out a Ministry Interest Form on our website, pick one up at the Welcome Center on Sundays, or contact the church office. Someone from our staff will follow up with you to discuss your interests, gifts, and the available opportunities.

What should I wear to church?

▼

We don't have a dress code at Grace Community Church. Some people dress casually in jeans and t-shirts, while others prefer to dress more formally. We invite you to come as you are and dress in a way that is comfortable for you.

We'd Love to Meet You

Whether you're exploring faith, looking for a church home, or interested in serving, we invite you to join us for a Sunday service or contact us with any questions. Our doors are open to everyone!
