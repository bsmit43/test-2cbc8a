Worship Ministry

Our worship ministry is dedicated to creating an atmosphere where people can encounter God through music and worship. We believe that worship is a powerful way to express our love and gratitude to God and to prepare our hearts to receive His Word.

Our worship teams include vocalists, instrumentalists, sound technicians, and visual arts specialists who work together to facilitate meaningful worship experiences.

Ministry Opportunities:

  • Traditional Choir (rehearsals on Thursday evenings)
  • Contemporary Worship Team (rehearsals on Wednesday evenings)
  • Orchestra and Instrumental Ensemble
  • Sound and Lighting Team
  • Visual Media Team
Join the Worship Team

Children's Ministry

Our children's ministry is committed to partnering with parents to help children develop a strong foundation of faith. We provide a safe, nurturing environment where children can learn about God's love and develop a personal relationship with Jesus Christ.

Our age-appropriate programs incorporate Bible teaching, worship, games, crafts, and other activities designed to engage children and help them grow spiritually.

Ministry Programs:

  • Nursery (0-2 years) - Available during all services
  • Preschool (3-5 years) - Sunday mornings at 9:00 AM & 11:00 AM
  • Elementary (K-5th grade) - Sunday mornings at 9:00 AM & 11:00 AM
  • Wednesday Night Kids - Bible study and activities at 7:00 PM
  • Vacation Bible School - Annual summer program
Volunteer with Children

Youth Ministry

Our youth ministry exists to help students in 6th-12th grade develop a lifelong relationship with Jesus Christ. We create a welcoming community where teens can explore their faith, ask questions, build meaningful friendships, and discover their God-given purpose.

Our programs combine biblical teaching, worship, small groups, service opportunities, and fun activities that engage students and encourage spiritual growth.

Ministry Programs:

  • Sunday Morning Youth Bible Study - 9:00 AM
  • Wednesday Night Youth Group - 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM
  • Monthly Youth Events and Activities
  • Annual Youth Retreats and Camps
  • Mission Trips and Service Projects
  • Student Leadership Team
Join Youth Ministry

Adult Ministry

Our adult ministry focuses on helping adults of all ages grow in their faith through Bible study, community, and service. We offer a variety of opportunities for adults to connect with others, deepen their understanding of Scripture, and apply biblical principles to everyday life.

From small groups to Bible classes to special events, our adult ministry provides multiple pathways for spiritual growth and meaningful relationships.

Ministry Opportunities:

  • Sunday Morning Bible Classes - 9:00 AM
  • Life Groups - Meeting in homes throughout the week
  • Men's Ministry - Bible studies, breakfast meetings, retreats
  • Women's Ministry - Bible studies, events, retreats
  • Senior Adult Ministry - Activities, fellowship, service
  • Marriage Ministry - Classes and events for couples
Find a Small Group

Missions & Outreach

Our missions and outreach ministry is committed to sharing God's love both locally and globally. We believe that as followers of Christ, we are called to serve others and share the good news of Jesus with people in our community and around the world.

Through partnerships with local organizations, support of global missionaries, and direct involvement in service projects and mission trips, we seek to meet physical and spiritual needs while demonstrating God's love in practical ways.

Ministry Opportunities:

  • Local Outreach - Food pantry, homeless ministry, crisis response
  • Community Service Projects - Monthly opportunities to serve
  • Mission Trips - Domestic and international opportunities
  • Missionary Support - Prayer, correspondence, financial support
  • Evangelism Training - Learning to share your faith
  • Disaster Relief Team - Responding to community needs
Get Involved in Missions

Prayer Ministry

Our prayer ministry believes in the power of prayer to transform lives, heal relationships, and bring about spiritual renewal. We are committed to praying for our church, community, and world, knowing that prayer is essential to our relationship with God and His work in our lives.

Through various prayer initiatives, we seek to create a culture of prayer within our church and equip people to develop a vibrant prayer life.

Ministry Opportunities:

  • Weekly Prayer Meeting - Wednesday, 6:00 AM
  • Sunday Morning Prayer Team - Before and during services
  • Prayer Chain - Responding to urgent prayer needs
  • Intercessory Prayer Team - Dedicated to specific prayer focuses
  • Prayer Walking - Praying throughout our community
  • Prayer Ministry Training - Learning to pray effectively
Join the Prayer Team
